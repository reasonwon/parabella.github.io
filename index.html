Para Bella Labs

We use games to prepare AI for future wars with humans, not instead of them.

What We Do

Para Bella Labs builds tactical simulation systems that prepare humans to make better decisions in machine-on-machine warfare.

What We Don’t Do

  • We do not build weapons
  • We do not design autonomous kill decisions
  • We do not replace human judgment

Humans remain in control. Our systems prepare options — humans decide.

Ludobella (Ludy)

Ludobella is our core tactical simulation engine that combines game-derived decision patterns with scenario-based simulation.
